About Me

Hello my name is Andrew Kim, I grew up on the Makah Reservation. I moved to Seattle in 2016 and attended North Seattle College's CS course through CS 142. Outside of school I spent the busy seasons helping with my parents' seafood business filling various roles from purchasing and processing to reporting fish tickets to Fish and Wildlife. My biggest role when I wasn't helping with the paperwork was grading the fish as it was being packaged to be shipped out to our customers. My current goal is to reach a point where I am ready to start interviewing for software development positions.
