About Me

Hello my name is Andrew Kim, I grew up on the Makah Reservation. I moved to Seattle in 2016 and attended North Seattle College's CS course through CS 142. Outside of school I spent the busy seasons helping with my parents' seafood business filling various roles from purchasing and processing to reporting fish tickets. My biggest role when I wasn't helping with the paperwork was grading the fish as it was being packaged to be shipped out to our customers. If I've learned one thing from my experience in QA, it would be the importance of understanding the end consumers needs. The only opinion that matters about the quality of a product is the client's, so it is imperitive to check-in from time to time to ensure that a project is coming along to spec. My current goal is to reach a point in technical ability to where I can start applying the skills I've already learned in a software development setting.
